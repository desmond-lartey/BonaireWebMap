Spatial data for your GIS
===============================

Source: OpenStreetMap (https://osm.org) is a database of open spatial data and a community of mappers who keep it up-to-date.
You can get this data in a convenient form with our service: https://data.nextgis.com

Each dataset contains:

* layers of geodata (data folder)  
* symbol icons for QGIS legend (svg folder)  
* ready-made project file for your GIS
